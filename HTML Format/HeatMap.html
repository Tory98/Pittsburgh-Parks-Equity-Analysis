These Maps Demonstrate Where Park Visitors Are Coming From Using Deciles

A decile represents a ranked 10% of visitors. For example, being in decile 10 means the block group is in the top 10% highest visting block groups. Being in decile 1 means the block group is in the lowest 10% of visiting block groups. A gray block group means that no data was recorded for that block group.
