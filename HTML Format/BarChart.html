These bar charts demonstrate the importance of being within a 20 minute walk of a park.

Each bar represents the average visit rate for block groups that fall within a 0 to 20, 20 to 40, or 40 to 60 minute walk of each park.

The trend clearly demonstrated by these bar charts is that block groups within a 20 minute walk of the park have a significantly higher visit rate than block groups that are more than a 20 minute walk away.

Two parks do not demonstrate this trend as clearly: Mellon Park and August Wilson. This suggests that there are more people taking alternate transit or willing to take a longer walk to visit those parks. These are a unique cases and a clear diversion from our other eight parks.
